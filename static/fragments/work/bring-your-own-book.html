Bring Your Own Book

  • projectCross-platform digital tabletop game
  • roleProgramming
  • toolsLua, HTML/CSS, JavaScript
  • websitewww.bringyourownbook.com

Bring Your Own Book is the game of borrowed phrases, where players search through books for text to take way out of context.

I collaborated with Matthew Moore and Luke Nalker to develop a digital version of the tabletop game, which is coming soon to iOS and Android.
