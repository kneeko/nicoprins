By Hand & Eye

  • roleAnimation and programming
  • toolsAdobe Flash, ActionScript, HTML/CSS

I produced a series of instructional animations to accompany the book, By Hand & Eye, written by Jim Tolpin and George R. Walker and published by Lost Art Press. The 29 animations demonstrate geometry techniques ranging from bisecting an angle to developing semi-elliptical curves using only an unmarked compass and straightedge.

I also wrote a custom player for the animations that allowed the user to jump between numbered steps which made following along with the animations much easier.
