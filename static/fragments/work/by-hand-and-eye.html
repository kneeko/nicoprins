By Hand & Eye

  • roleAnimation and programming
  • toolsAdobe Flash, ActionScript, HTML/CSS

I animated a series of instructional animations to accompany the book, By Hand & Eye, written by Jim Tolpin and George R. Walker and published by Lost Art Press. The animations demonstrate archaic geometry techniques ranging from bisecting an angle to developing semi-elliptical curves using an unmarked compass and straightedge.

I also wrote a custom player that allowed the user to move back and forth through the animation step by step.
