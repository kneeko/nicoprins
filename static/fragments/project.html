oh my giraffe

  • roledeveloper and designer
  • toolsLua, Obj-C, Java, HTML/CSS, JavaScript

I began working on oh my giraffe to get programming experience beyond web development. Little did I know that I would find myself absorbed into game design, prototyping, and marketing as well.

Through the support of some exceptional mentors, I was able to see it through to a fantastic release and was lucky enough to get mentions on TouchArcade, Pocket Gamer, Gamezebo and others. I released simultaneously for iOS and Android in addition to offering a free preview for Windows, Mac OS X and Linux.

As well as the game itself, I created a promotional website, video teaser and stills for use on the App Store, Google Play and the Amazon Appstore.
